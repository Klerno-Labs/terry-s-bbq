THE PITMASTER

TERRY FOSTER

"I started smoking meat in the backyard of my Louisville home in '85. Folks kept knocking on the door asking what smelled so good. Thirty years later, we're still doing it the same way—low heat, good wood, and plenty of patience. We don't use gas, and we don't cut corners. If it takes 14 hours to get right, we let it go 14 hours."
