A Story of Smoke

Terry Foster started cooking in a backyard shed in 1985 with nothing but a smoker made from an old oil tank and a dream. Thirty years later, we're still serving the same recipes, with the same patience, in our "Old Western" tavern style hall.

We believe BBQ isn't just food; it's an event. It's the wait, the smell of the smoke, and the community around the table. Come sit a spell, grab a bourbon, and let us feed you like family.

- Terry Foster, Pitmaster
